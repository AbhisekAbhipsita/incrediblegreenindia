We are a group of young and responsible citizens of nature carrying out a mission for the betterment of the society. The main objective of this organization is to spread awareness, share similar interests, encourage people and help plants trees. We collect money and plant trees in dry lands in an effort to increase the natural rainfall of that surrounding which would thereby increase the ground water level and cause natural rains which lead to the formation of lakes etc. Increasing climatic temperatures is a serious concern. Plantation of trees helps control the local temperatures and brings equilibrium to the surrounding eco-system. Plantation also increases the fertility of the soil, raises the employment factor, increase awareness in people to take up responsible and responsive agriculture, our initial mission is to plant a minimum of one plant a day which we believe is a humble beginning. The advantages of planting a single tree are innumerable and beyond comparison. There are stories of people who have succeeded in sprouting entire forests single handedly; the potential that lies in a combined effort is beyond imagination.
